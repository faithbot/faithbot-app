WordPress Design and Development

Resource Energy Solutions wanted a refresh of their company website. It was important that it be developed quickly, easy to modify and update, and focused on promoting the company's products.

The website was built starting with the "Enfold" theme, which has many built-in features allowing me to quickly and easily build attractive pages. I then used some custom CSS to make adjustments to the design where the default style did not suit the intended look and feel of the page.

Content-First Design

I started the design based on content I wrote myself, with a strong focus on eye-catching phrases that focused on the most prominent features of the products. I then encouraged the company to review and edit the content to suit their ideas of what messages to promote. This made it easier for the content to be developed with strong, focused marketing messages while ensuring the design was complimentary to the content.
