Tech Tips

Heres an assortment of interesting tech tips!

Don't Clean your Screen with Windex

It makes glass shiny and transparent, but don't use this common household cleaner on any electronic screens. The ammonia in Windex can damage your screen. Instead, treat your electronic screens like a delicate pair of eyeglasses. Apply a small amount of a mixture of half vinegar, half water to a microfiber cleaning cloth and gently wipe the surface. Be careful not to let water drip down into the insides of the screen. You can also buy special cleaning solutions- often the same solutions used for eyeglasses are also safe for electronic screens.

Use Your Computer Without a Mouse

Did your wireless mouse just die, and you can't find a battery? Or maybe someone borrowed it, and never returned it! Don't panic, because you can use keyboard shortcuts to do nearly anything online.
